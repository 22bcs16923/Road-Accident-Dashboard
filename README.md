🚗 Road Accident Dashboard
📌 Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze road accident data to identify patterns in accident severity, vehicle involvement, and environmental conditions, helping improve road safety and decision-making.

📊 About Dataset

The dataset contains road accident records including:

Accident severity (Fatal, Serious, Slight)
Vehicle types involved
Road types
Weather and wind conditions
Light conditions (daylight/darkness)
Urban vs Rural locations
Monthly trends across years

This data helps in understanding accident causes and identifying high-risk factors.

Data Visualization

🔍 Key Insights
1. Accident Severity Analysis
Majority of accidents are Slight (~84%)
Serious accidents account for ~15%
Fatal accidents are very low (~2%)
2. Vehicle Type Analysis
Cars contribute the highest number of casualties
Bikes and goods vehicles also have significant involvement
Heavy vehicles contribute less comparatively
3. Road Type Analysis
Single carriageways account for the majority of accidents
Dual carriageways and roundabouts have fewer incidents
Slip roads and unknown categories contribute minimally
4. Road Condition Analysis
Most accidents occur on dry roads
Wet roads contribute a smaller share
Snow and extreme conditions have minimal impact
5. Urban vs Rural Analysis
Rural areas have more casualties than urban areas
Indicates higher risk due to speed and road conditions
6. Light Condition Analysis
Most accidents occur during daylight
Darkness accounts for a smaller portion
7. Time Trend Analysis
Accidents are consistent throughout the year
No extreme seasonal variation observed
